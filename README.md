## 🚀 About

Program is building the next generation of higher education.

Our mission is to create a single platform where university students can access high-quality courses, collaborate with peers, track their academic progress, and prepare for successful careers.

---

## ✨ Vision

To become the leading education platform for university students across the Middle East and beyond.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Empower every student with accessible, engaging, and career-focused education.

---

## 🌍 What We're Building

- Modern Learning Platform
- Course Marketplace
- Student Dashboard
- Instructor Portal
- Admin Console
- Progress Tracking
- Assignments & Quizzes
- Career & Community Features
